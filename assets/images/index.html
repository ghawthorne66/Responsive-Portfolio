About Me

My Name is Christopher Anthony Kalla. I like anything that has to do wth music. I started playing guitar when I was 10 years old and have never stopped learning and playing new instruments.

After a few yeaers of learning guitar my passion grew to allow me to join a group of friends and start playing out live. This later led me to travel the World sharing my music with others. This was one of the best experiences of my life!
